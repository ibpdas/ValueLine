Methodology – How to read this dashboard

Model explanation, flow and notes.

How the framework works

This dashboard uses a three level causal model showing how Level 1 foundational enablers influence Level 2 maturity and Level 3 outcomes. It is not a benchmark. Performance data is illustrative and does not represent any real organisation.

Understanding the three levels

Level 1 foundations are the conditions directly under organisational control such as strategy, ownership, standards, community and engagement.

Level 2 maturity emerges from foundations over time and covers uses, data, leadership, culture, tools and skills.

Level 3 outcomes represent public value including savings, satisfaction, efficiency and time saved.

Indicator types

  • Leading indicators show early progress on foundations.
  • Signal indicators reflect emerging maturity and adoption.
  • Lagging indicators show realised benefits and outcomes.

Methodology and evidence

  • Delivery artefacts, trackers and governance papers for Level 1.
  • Maturity assessments, surveys and engagement insight for Level 2.
  • Verified benefits, reuse metrics and public value indicators for Level 3.

Caveats

This model simplifies complex systems and is intended for strategic discussion and learning, not audit or assurance. Outcomes lag behind foundations and maturity. Desired outcomes inform future foundational design.

This methodology is part of the ValueLine data to impact model. Model department only, not real performance data.
